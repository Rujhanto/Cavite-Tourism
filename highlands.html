Highlands & Ridges

Escape the heat and enjoy cool mountain air with breathtaking views of Taal Volcano in Tagaytay.

Taal Volcano View (Tagaytay)

One of the most iconic views in the Philippines—a volcano within a lake, within an island, within a lake. The Tagaytay Ridge offers a panoramic perspective of this geological wonder.

Sky Ranch Tagaytay

An amusement park perched on the ridge with a spectacular Ferris wheel and rides that give you a stunning aerial view of Taal Lake and the surrounding mountains.

Sonya's Garden (Alfonso)

A lush garden retreat in Alfonso, blending organic farm, spa, and restaurant. A beloved highland escape known for its romantic atmosphere and farm-fresh cuisine.

Anya Resort (Tagaytay)

A luxury resort offering stunning views of Taal Lake, infinity pools, and world-class spa facilities nestled in the cool Tagaytay highlands.
