Island Hopping & Coastal Tours

Set sail to explore hidden coves, historical fortresses, and the crystal-clear waters surrounding Cavite.

Corregidor Island

"The Rock" is a must-visit for history buffs and nature lovers alike. While it's a historical shrine, the boat ride from Cavite City offers stunning views of the bay and access to secluded beaches on the island's tail.

Carabao Island (Fort Frank)

Located off the coast of Ternate, this island was once a fortified defense point. Today, it is a popular stop for boat tours, offering rugged rock formations and clear waters perfect for snorkeling and diving.

El Fraile Island (Fort Drum)

Often called the "Concrete Battleship," this unique island was leveled and covered in concrete to look like a ship. It is a marvel of military engineering and a striking sight to pass during a coastal boat tour.

Limbones Island & Coves

A favorite for local campers and divers, Limbones features calm coves and rich marine biodiversity. The boat ride through the Maragondon coastline reveals dramatic cliffs and hidden pocket beaches.
