Farm Tourism

Pick fresh strawberries, harvest organic veggies, and experience authentic farm-to-table dining in Silang and Amadeo.

Gourmet Farms (Silang)

One of the most popular agritourism destinations, Gourmet Farms is home to the famous "Sonya's Garden." It offers herb gardens, organic produce picking, and a full-service restaurant.

Organic Vegetable Farms (Amadeo)

The cool climate of Amadeo makes it perfect for growing vegetables like lettuce, broccoli, and cabbage. Visitors can join harvesting activities and learn about organic farming methods.

Silang Farmers' Market

A bustling market at the gateway to the highlands where local farmers sell seasonal fruits, vegetables, and fresh produce straight from the fields at very affordable prices.

Themed Farm Stays

Experience "Glamping" or stay in rustic wooden cabins within active farms. Wake up to the sound of nature and enjoy fresh, farm-harvested breakfasts.
