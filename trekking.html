Mountain Trekking

Conquer the summits of Cavite and witness breathtaking 360-degree views of the archipelago.

Mt. Pico de Loro (Ternate)

Named after its "Parrot's Beak" shape, this is one of the most popular hiking destinations near Manila. The summit offers a stunning view of the South China Sea and the nearby mountains of Batangas.

Mt. Marami (Magallanes)

Known for its unique rocky formations called "Silyang Bato," Mt. Marami offers a moderately challenging climb with quiet, scenic trails and impressive stone towers at the peak.

Mt. Buntis (Maragondon)

This mountain is steeped in Philippine history as it is closely linked to the story of Andres Bonifacio. The trail is relatively easy, making it great for beginners who want a mix of history and nature.

Mt. Nagpatong (Maragondon)

Located near Mt. Buntis, Mt. Nagpatong features rock-heavy terrain and is the site of the Bonifacio Memorial Shrine, where trekkers can reflect on the province's revolutionary past.
