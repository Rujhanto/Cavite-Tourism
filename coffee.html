Coffee Culture

Taste the legendary Kapeng Barako and discover why Cavite is the coffee processing capital of the Philippines.

Amadeo Coffee Festival

Held annually in Amadeo, this festival celebrates the municipality's role as the top coffee-producing area. It features farm tours, cupping sessions, and local barista competitions showcasing Kapeng Barako.

Kapeng Barako (Amadeo & Alfonso)

The bold and full-bodied Liberica coffee variety grown in the highlands of Cavite. Kapeng Barako has a distinctive aroma and is a symbol of local identity—best enjoyed black with a local kakanin.

Coffee Farm Tours

Visit the working coffee farms of Amadeo and Silang to see the full bean-to-cup process: picking, pulping, drying, and roasting. Many farms offer immersive tours and farm-fresh brews.

Specialty Cafes (Tagaytay & Silang)

The highlands are home to a growing specialty coffee scene. Garden cafes and roasteries along Aguinaldo Highway serve single-origin brews with Taal Volcano as the backdrop.
