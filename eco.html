Eco-Adventure

Zipline through forests or explore wildlife sanctuaries dedicated to preserving Cavite's biodiversity.

Forest Zipline Adventures

Several eco-parks in Cavite offer exhilarating zipline rides through dense forest canopies. Feel the rush of speed while taking in the stunning green landscapes of the highlands.

Eco-Parks & Nature Reserves

Cavite has several protected eco-parks that serve as sanctuaries for endemic flora and fauna. Guided nature walks reveal the province's rich biodiversity hidden in its forested mountains.

Bird Watching & Wildlife

The forested mountains of Maragondon and Ternate are home to various bird species and wildlife. Guided bird-watching tours are available for nature enthusiasts.

Coastal Mangrove Tours

The coastal municipalities of Naic and Kawit feature mangrove reserves. Kayak or boat tours through these ecosystems offer a unique perspective on Cavite's coastal ecology.
