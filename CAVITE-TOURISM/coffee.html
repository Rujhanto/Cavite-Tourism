Coffee Culture

Savor the aroma of the heritage beans that made Cavite the coffee processing capital of the country.

Amadeo: The Coffee Capital

Amadeo is home to the largest land area devoted to coffee in the province. It is famous for the "Pahimis" blend, a unique combination of Robusta, Arabica, Excelsa, and Liberica (Barako) beans.

The Liberica (Barako) Legacy

Cavite is one of the few places in the world that produces Liberica, locally known as "Barako." It is prized for its strong, pungent aroma and distinct woody, smoky flavor profile.

Silang Artisan Cafes

Silang has become a hub for third-generation coffee shops and garden cafes. These spots offer a modern twist on local beans, providing cozy environments for coffee enthusiasts to relax.

From Bean to Cup

Experience the traditional sun-drying process and modern roasting facilities. Cavite serves as a vital processing hub, ensuring that the heritage of Philippine coffee reaches every cup.
