Highlands & Ridges

Breathe in the cool mountain air and witness the majestic vistas of Southern Luzon.

Tagaytay Ridge

Famous for its breathtaking view of Taal Volcano and Lake, the ridge offers a refreshing climate year-round. It is lined with restaurants and parks, making it the perfect spot for sightseeing and relaxation.

People's Park in the Sky

Originally an unfinished mansion for a visiting dignitary, this park is the highest point in Cavite. On clear days, you can see as far as Manila Bay and the surrounding mountains of Batangas.

Tagaytay Picnic Grove

A classic destination for families, offering eco-trails, ziplines, and horseback riding. The wooden boardwalk provides one of the most accessible and stunning views of the volcano.

Mount Sungay

Often referred to as the "Mountain of the Horn," this peak is part of the Taal caldera rim. It serves as a natural barrier and provides a rugged, scenic backdrop to the Tagaytay landscape.
