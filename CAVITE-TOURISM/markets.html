Markets & Pasalubong

Explore the heart of local commerce and take home a piece of Cavite through its famous markets.

Mahogany Market (Tagaytay)

A legendary stop for foodies and travelers. Famous for its fresh beef section and the "Bulalo" row, this market also offers a wide array of local honey, plants, and traditional wooden handicrafts.

Silang Farmers' Market

The gateway to the highlands, this market is where you can find the best prices for seasonal fruits like pineapple, dragon fruit, and lanzones. It is a bustling hub for fresh agricultural produce straight from the fields.

Cavite City Public Market

For the freshest seafood in the province, this coastal market is unbeatable. From blue crabs and mussels to a wide variety of dried fish (daing), it showcases the rich bounty of Manila Bay.

Specialty Pasalubong Centers

Located along the main highways of Aguinaldo and Tagaytay-Nasugbu, these centers are one-stop shops for Cavite's famous coffee beans, Jacob's Buko Pie, and Samala's rice cakes.
