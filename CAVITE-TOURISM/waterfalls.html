Nature Waterfalls

Cool down in the natural spring pools and hidden cascades of Cavite's verdant woodlands.

Balite Falls (Amadeo)

A serene destination featuring two falls flowing into a deep natural pool. It is surrounded by bamboo trees and local cottages, making it a popular spot for a traditional Filipino family picnic.

Pantihan Falls (Maragondon)

Also known as Balayungan Falls, this site is famous for its wide, curtain-like cascade. It offers a raw, untouched nature experience for those willing to travel a bit off the beaten path.

Maliclic Falls (General Aguinaldo)

A hidden treasure tucked away in the mountains of Bailen. It is known for its tall, slender drop and very cold, refreshing water that comes straight from the upland springs.

Palace Falls (Dasmariñas)

Located near the city center, this smaller waterfall is part of an eco-park project. It provides a quick nature break for residents and visitors who don't have time for a full mountain trek.
