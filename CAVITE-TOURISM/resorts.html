Staycations & Resorts

Find your home away from home—from luxury ridge-side hotels to cozy garden villas.

Luxury Highlands Hotels

Tagaytay offers premier hotels with infinity pools and private balconies that overlook the Taal Volcano. Perfect for anniversaries and special occasions where luxury and scenery meet.

Hidden Garden Villas (Alfonso & Silang)

For those seeking privacy, these boutique villas offer a "sanctuary" vibe. Often surrounded by flower gardens and pine trees, they provide a peaceful, cool-climate escape for families and couples.

Family Water Resorts (Tanza & Naic)

Cavite's lowlands are famous for large-scale resorts featuring giant pools and slides. These are the go-to spots for "Barkada" outings and large family reunions during the summer months.

Themed Farm Stays

Experience "Glamping" or stay in rustic wooden cabins within active farms. These resorts offer a unique experience where you can wake up to the sound of nature and enjoy fresh, farm-harvested breakfasts.
