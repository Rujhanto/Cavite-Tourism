Coastal Escapes

Unwind by the gray sands and blue waters of Cavite’s historic coastline.

Marine Base (Boracay de Cavite)

Located within the Gregorio Lim Marine Base in Ternate, this beach is famous for its fine sand and clear waters. It is a favorite weekend spot for families looking for a clean, secure cove surrounded by lush hills.

Puerto Azul (Paniman Bay)

Once known as the "World's Best Resort Cluster," Puerto Azul remains a scenic destination. Its rugged cliffs and hidden coves offer a peaceful escape for those who want to see the wilder side of Cavite's shores.

Naic Beachfronts

Naic offers long stretches of coastline lined with local resorts. It is well-known for being a top spot for "Luto sa Bilao" beach picnics, where visitors can enjoy fresh seafood right by the water.

Caylabne Bay

A secluded Mediterranean-style resort cove in Ternate. Surrounded by a tropical rainforest, it provides a high-end coastal experience with calm waters perfect for kayaking and sunset viewing.
