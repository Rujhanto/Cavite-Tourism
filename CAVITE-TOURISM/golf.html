Golf & Recreation

Experience world-class greens and elite recreational facilities set against Cavite's rolling landscapes.

Eagle Ridge Golf & Country Club (Gen. Trias)

One of the largest golfing facilities in the country, featuring four championship courses designed by golf legends like Greg Norman and Nick Faldo. It offers a diverse playing experience for both pros and enthusiasts.

The Riviera Golf Club (Silang)

Nestled in the cool highlands of Silang, this club provides a refreshing climate for a full day of golf. Its scenic fairways are meticulously maintained and offer a peaceful escape from the city heat.

Sherwood Hills Golf Club (Trece Martires)

Known for its unique inland links-style layout, Sherwood Hills is consistently ranked as one of the best courses in the Philippines. It challenges golfers with its strategic bunkering and undulating greens.

Elite Country Club Recreation

Beyond the fairways, these clubs offer premium recreational amenities including Olympic-sized swimming pools, tennis courts, and grand ballrooms, providing a complete lifestyle experience for members and guests.
