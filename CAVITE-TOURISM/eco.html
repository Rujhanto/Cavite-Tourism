Eco-Adventure

Push your limits and explore the wild side of Cavite through exhilarating outdoor activities.

Tagaytay Ridge Zipline

Experience the thrill of flying over the lush valleys of Tagaytay. These ziplines offer a unique aerial perspective of the Taal Volcano and the surrounding forest canopy at high speeds.

Cavite Wildlife Sanctuary

A protected area dedicated to preserving the province's native flora and fauna. Visitors can take guided eco-walks to spot endemic bird species and learn about local conservation efforts.

Forest Adventure Courses

Tucked away in the cooler regions of Silang and Alfonso, these courses feature rope bridges, wall climbing, and rappelling. They are designed to challenge both physical strength and mental focus.

Off-Road ATV Trails

Navigate through muddy paths, river crossings, and hilly terrains on an All-Terrain Vehicle. These trails provide an exciting way to see the rugged landscapes of Cavite's interior towns.
