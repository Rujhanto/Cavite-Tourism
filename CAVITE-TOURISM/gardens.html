Garden Restaurants

Dine amidst blooming flowers and lush greenery for a truly refreshing culinary experience.

Sonya's Garden (Alfonso)

A pioneer in the "Eat, Pray, Love" lifestyle in Cavite. Known for its farm-to-table salads with edible flowers and freshly baked bread, all served in a dreamy, romantic conservatory filled with greenery.

Marcia Adams (Alfonso)

Inspired by the gardens of Tuscany and Provence, this restaurant is tucked away in a hidden corner of Cavite. It features stone-walled dining rooms and a lush landscape, perfect for a Mediterranean-style lunch.

Balay Dako (Tagaytay)

While known for its Filipino buffet, the open-air "Terraza" and garden-style architecture allow diners to enjoy the cool breeze. It captures the feel of a traditional Filipino mansion set within a sprawling estate.

Shambala Living Museum (Silang)

More than just a restaurant, this is a sanctuary for art and culture. Diners can enjoy local delicacies in a garden filled with indigenous Filipino sculptures and traditional "Ifugao" huts.
