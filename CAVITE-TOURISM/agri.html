Farm Tourism

Reconnect with nature through sustainable farming and fresh, organic harvests.

Agri-Leisure Hubs

Cavite is a pioneer in farm-to-table concepts. Many farms in Silang and Alfonso allow guests to pick their own vegetables and enjoy meals prepared with ingredients harvested right from the field.

The Pineapple Trail

Tagaytay and Silang are famous for their sweet "Formosa" pineapples. Travelers can visit vast plantations to learn about the cultivation process and buy fresh produce directly from local farmers.

Organic & Herb Gardens

Explore specialized farms dedicated to culinary and medicinal herbs. These sites often hold workshops on urban gardening and composting, promoting a sustainable and healthy lifestyle.

Interactive Animal Farms

Perfect for families, these farms offer hands-on experiences with livestock. From feeding goats to watching traditional poultry raising, it provides an educational look at the province's pastoral life.
