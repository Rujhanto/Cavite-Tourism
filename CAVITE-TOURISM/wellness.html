Wellness & Spas

Surrender to tranquility and rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul in Cavite's premier healing sanctuaries.

Nurture Wellness Village (Tagaytay)

A pioneer in wellness tourism, this village offers "Ifugao-inspired" spa treatments and holistic healing. It is famous for its "Amu'ray" massage and organic gardens that provide a truly Filipino relaxation experience.

Qi Wellness Living

Inspired by ancient Chinese healing traditions, this tea house and spa overlooks the Taal Lake. It combines Yin-Yang philosophies with modern hydrotherapy to create a deeply meditative and relaxing atmosphere.

Anya Resort Wellness

A luxury retreat in Silang that focuses on the "Three Pillars of Wellness": Body, Mind, and Spirit. It offers personalized spa journeys, quiet library spaces, and curated wellness menus for a complete lifestyle reset.

Sonya's Garden Secret Spa

Tucked away in Alfonso, this garden spa uses essential oils and herbs harvested directly from their backyard. The soothing sound of fountains and the scent of blooming flowers make it a favorite for nature lovers.
