Theme Parks & Amusement

From sky-high Ferris wheels to refreshing water slides, find your next adrenaline rush here.

Sky Ranch Tagaytay

Home to the "Sky Eye," one of the tallest Ferris wheels in the country. This park offers a variety of family-friendly rides and a spectacular view of the Taal Volcano while you're suspended in the air.

Aqua Mira Waterpark (Tanza)

A massive water park located within the Tanza Oasis Hotel and Resort. It features giant slides, a wave pool, and various interactive water play areas, perfect for escaping the summer heat.

Paradizoo Theme Farm (Mendez)

A unique combination of a zoo and a theme farm. It offers educational interactions with animals, beautiful flower gardens, and "ecotainment" activities that are great for school field trips and family outings.

Torres Farm and Resort (Naic)

Known for its "Miniature World" theme, this park features scaled-down replicas of famous world landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Merlion. It's a popular spot for photography and "around the world" themed tours.
