Modern Museums & Galleries

Immerse yourself in contemporary Filipino artistry and meticulously curated local history.

Museo Orlina (Tagaytay)

Showcasing the brilliant glass sculptures of internationally acclaimed artist Ramon Orlina. The museum features indoor galleries and an outdoor sculpture garden with a stunning view of Taal Lake.

Museo de La Salle (Dasmariñas)

Located within DLSU-D, this museum is dedicated to the preservation of the 19th-century Philippine "Illustrado" lifestyle. It houses an extensive collection of antique furniture, ceramics, and religious artifacts.

Puzzle Mansion (Tagaytay)

Once holding a Guinness World Record for the largest collection of jigsaw puzzles, this unique museum features thousands of completed puzzles ranging from classic paintings to 3D architectural models.

Gen. Emilio Aguinaldo Museum

A modernized museum that uses interactive displays to tell the story of the Philippine Revolution. It houses the original first Philippine Flag and personal memorabilia of the first President.
