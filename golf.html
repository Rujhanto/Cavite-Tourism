Golf & Recreation

Tee off at world-class golf courses in the "Golf Capital of the Philippines."

Sherwood Hills Golf & Country Club

One of the premier golf clubs in Cavite, Sherwood Hills offers a beautifully landscaped course with challenging holes and stunning views. The club facilities include a clubhouse, restaurant, and pool.

The Orchard Golf & Country Club

A prestigious 36-hole golf club in Dasmariñas. Known for its well-maintained fairways, lush surroundings, and comprehensive sports facilities including tennis courts and a swimming pool.

Splendido Taal Golf Club

Overlooking the majestic Taal Lake, this 18-hole championship course offers one of the most scenic golfing experiences in Asia. Every hole presents a unique challenge set against a breathtaking natural backdrop.

Alta Vista Golf & Country Club

A championship golf course in Silang offering a world-class golfing experience surrounded by cool highland air. The club features elegant facilities and a welcoming atmosphere for golfers of all levels.
