Garden Restaurants

Dine surrounded by orchids and lush greenery in the many "secret garden" cafes found in Silang.

Hidden Garden Cafes (Silang)

Silang is dotted with charming garden cafes tucked behind lush greenery and flower arrangements. These are perfect spots for a leisurely brunch or afternoon coffee surrounded by nature.

Sonya's Garden (Alfonso)

The legendary garden restaurant and spa in Alfonso. Famous for its organic salad bar, herbal teas, and romantic ambiance among flowering plants and a bubbling fountain.

Gourmet Farms Restaurant

The restaurant arm of Gourmet Farms serves dishes made with produce harvested fresh from their gardens. The farm-to-table concept is in its purest form here.

Ridge View Restaurants (Tagaytay)

Several restaurants along the Tagaytay Ridge offer alfresco dining with a view of Taal Lake. From casual carinderia to fine dining, the scenery elevates every meal.
