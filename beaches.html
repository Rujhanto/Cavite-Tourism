Coastal Escapes

Explore the sandy shores of Ternate and Naic. Perfect for a quick weekend beach getaway near Manila.

Ternate Beaches

The municipality of Ternate features calm cove beaches facing Manila Bay. The area is perfect for swimming, kayaking, and enjoying fresh seafood from local fishing villages.

Corregidor Island Beaches

Beyond its historical significance, Corregidor Island offers secluded beaches along its southern coast. The boat ride from Cavite City provides stunning views of Manila Bay.

Limbones Island Coves

A favorite for local campers and divers, Limbones features calm coves and rich marine biodiversity off the Maragondon coastline, with dramatic cliffs and hidden pocket beaches.

Carabao Island (Fort Frank)

Located off the coast of Ternate, this island offers rugged rock formations and clear waters perfect for snorkeling and diving, alongside its historical military fortifications.
